how to book a session

Please read all the following terms carefully and if you’re ready to make an appointment - email us using the link at the end of this page. Don’t forget to include all the information and pictures we ask for, otherwise, we will not be able to get back to you.

I charge $1000 for a one-day session. At this moment we only accept cash as a payment method. Payments for tattoo sessions are due immediately at the end of each session.

A deposit is necessary to confirm your appointment - this is $100. Deposits are non-refundable. The deposit can be paid in cash or online using Zelle, Venmo or CashApp

The deposit serves to reserve all your booking dates and will be applied to the final tattoo session if the project requires multiple sessions. If it is a single session, it will be applied to that instead.

If you need to change the date of your appointment due to unforeseen circumstances, you must let us know at least 7 days prior to the session so we can rearrange our schedules. A new deposit will be required to continue working together if you cancel or reschedule with less than 7 days’ notice. You can reschedule your appointment only once without losing your deposit.
